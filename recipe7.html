Kheer

Ingredients

  • ½ cup basmati rice or any long grain rice
  • 8 cups whole milk
  • A pinch of saffron
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • 1¼ cups sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • 1 tbsp chopped cashews
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • 1 tbsp chopped pistachio nuts

Preparation

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly in cold water, drain, and keep aside.
  2. In a heavy-bottomed saucepan, bring the milk to a boil over moderately high heat. Then reduce the heat to low and stirring often cook for 45 minutes.
  3. Add the rice and continue to stir often until the milk is reduced to almost 2/3 of its original volume and the rice is fully cooked.
  4. Add the saffron and cardamom. Stir in the sweetened condensed milk. Remove from heat after 5 minutes.
  5. Heat ghee in a small sauté-pan over moderate heat, fry the cashews until golden. Remove cashews from the pan, and in the remaining ghee fry the raisins until they swell up and then add both cashews and raisins to the sweet rice.
  6. Serve hot or cold garnished with chopped pistachios.

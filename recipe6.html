Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk - 4 cups
  • Lemon juice - 2 tbsp + 1 tbsp water
  • Sugar - 1 ½ cups
  • Water - 2 ½ cups
  • Rose essence - 2 tsp

Preparation

  1. Boil the milk in medium flame stirring occasionally so that it doesn’t get burned at the bottom.
  2. Add the mixture of lemon juice and water (2 tbsp + 1 tbsp) slowly to the boiling milk to get the whey.
  3. Let the gas be in simmer as the whey gets separated, remove the pan from the gas and drain the whey alone with a cheesecloth.
  4. Wash the cheese with running water to remove the lemon flavor from it.
  5. Squeeze out all the water from the cheese cloth.
  6. Take all the whey out and rub the cheese with very little pressure.
  7. Take a small size of cheese and check if you can make balls without any scratch on it.
  8. Now split the whey or cheese into 12 equal pieces and make clear balls of it.
  9. In the meantime, mix the sugar and water in the pressure cooker and let it boil in medium flame. Once the sugar syrup starts boiling, put the cheese balls one by one into it and pressure cook until one whistle.
  10. Turn off the gas.
  11. Transfer the rasgullas one by one to another container and pour the sugar syrup also in that.
  12. Add the rose essence. Let it come to room temperature.
  13. Keep it in the refrigerator until it cools. And serve.

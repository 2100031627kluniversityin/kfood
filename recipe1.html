Apple Salad

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup (60 ml) plain yogurt or sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) chopped fresh mint
  • 3 tablespoons (45 ml) ground blanched almonds
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1 ml) crushed cardamom seeds
  • 2 tablespoons (30 ml) orange or lemon juice
  • 3 medium-sized apples, cored and diced
  • 1/2 cup (120 ml) seedless grapes, halved

Preparation

Follow these steps to prepare the Apple Salad:

  1. Cut tomatoes into fine pieces. Cook rice and allow it to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan, add seasonings. Fry for a few minutes, then add tomatoes and fry until it becomes soft.
  3. Continue frying in medium flame with the ground masala paste.
  4. Add salt and cooked rice.
  5. Fry on high flame until rice becomes hot.
  6. Garnish with cut coriander leaves and curry leaves.
